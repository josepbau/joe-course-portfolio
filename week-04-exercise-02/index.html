Dallas Cowboys Leading Pass-Catcher Jason Witten

Jason Witten | 2010 | NFL

Jason Witten played 17 seasons in the NFL and spent 16 of them with my Dallas Cowboys. He is the team's leading Pass-Catcher and will soon be inducted into the Hall of Fame in 2026. Jason Witten was also my late Father's favorite Dallas Cowboy player. My father was never one to wear jerseys but he would proudly were that #82 jersey with pride. Jason Witten wasn't just a great football, he was also a great human being with his philanthropy work off the field. In 2012 Jason Witten was awarded the Wallter Payton NFL Man of the Year Award for his philanthropic work with his foundation.
